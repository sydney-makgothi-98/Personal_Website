Problem

Modern CRM software are overpriced and overly complicated to get out of once you have paid for a subcription.

Small to medium sized businesses dveloping their client bases find themselves in need of way to keep track of their leads, without having to resort to EXCEL or even less sophisticated software. The app in question would also have to be intuiative and easy to use. The reason being, cost reduction, the users of the app should not have to concern themselves with having workshops to train employees on how to use the app.

Solution

The solution posed to the problem above is a much more afforable CRM software. Developed by MSS this CRM is easy to integrate with existing infrastcure allowing you to keep track of all your agents and their leads.
