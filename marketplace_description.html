Problem

We live in a digital age in which we can contact each other, friends, family, loved one's etc. However there still exist marketplaces where people meet sell and purchase goods at afforable prices.

The main problem here is that people most sellers in these congested areas have to commute long distances with large hauls of produce, clothing etc. Without the guarentee that any of their products will be sold. There are marketplace apps that have already been developed such as Shopify and Shein. However they require exuberant monthly fees for registration, up to $2300.

Solution

A marketplace app was developed for the sole purpose helping lower working class citizines integrate seemlessly, at virtually no cost into the digital world.

The app allows for users to register, and list the goods they have available for sale. Integrated with live message sending capabilities, buyers and sellers can contact one other directly, and negotiate with one another in the comfort of their own homes.
